<about-me>

My name is Rúben Duarte, I'm 29 years old, and I live in Porto, Portugal. I started my journey in the Geography field in 2012 right after I joined the Faculty of Arts and Humanities of Porto University. In 2016, after I completed my bachelor degree, I started my Master's in GIS and Spatial Planning.

Two years later, I started an internship in my local parish, where I developed an illustrated map using GIS and Illustration softwares.

After the internship, I joined Hidurbe Serviços, S.A., a company I stood for 4 years working as a Geographer and GIS Specialist. In my 4 years in this company, I developed many skills, such as field work (GPS surveys), risk mapping, network analysis, among others.

Recently, in 2022, I joined a new company (BizFuture) as a Geographer and GIS Specialist.
